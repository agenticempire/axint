# Beta Tester Seeding Program

## Strategy

Seed 10–15 influential developers 2 weeks before public launch. Their early feedback becomes:
1. Bug reports we fix before launch
2. Social proof (tweets, blog posts) on launch day
3. Real-world use cases for the README and docs

## Ask on Launch Day

For beta testers who engage, the ask is simple:
1. **One tweet** with their honest take (positive or constructive)
2. **One GitHub star** (if they find it useful)
3. **Optional**: A short quote we can feature ("Axint saved me X hours on my App Intents")
